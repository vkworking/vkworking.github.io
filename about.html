ABOUT US / OUR POLICIES

MADEHEART unites more than 100 craftsmen from Ukraine.

We get together to sell our products internationally. We produce products out of wood, clay, metal, textile, wool and other materials.

We are looking for reliable Partners in US, Germany, England and other countries.

Please, take a look at our policies!

DELIVERY POLICY

Buyer covers all delivery expenses. Delivery expenses are calculated automatically in the shopping cart. Buyer can come up with his own delivery method. In this case, the shipping cost is calculated individually. The production and delivery time of the Buyer’s order is calculated individually. We indicate this information in the invoice.

PACKING

The products do not have unique packs. We pack the items very carefully into cardboard paper, tighten them by stretch pellicle and put the products into the hard carton box. We can discuss the possibility of making unique boxes for the items if the order exceeds 100 pieces of the same item.

PAYMENT POLICY

Buyer has to make 100% pre-payment for the chosen products. You choose the products, add them to the shopping cart, change their quantity in the shopping cart, and request an invoice. We prepare the invoice in 24 hours and send it to Your e-mail. We will inform You as soon as we receive Your money transfer.

RETURN AND REFUND POLICY

The client has the right to return the goods within 14 calendar days from the moment of receiving. In this case, redelivery of the goods is paid at the expense of MADEheart.com. In other cases, money for the goods will not be returned.

COOKIE POLICY

We use cookies on our website to help us offer You the best online experience. We’ve already placed some of these little packets of the information on Your browser to do this. By continuing to use our website, You’re agreeing to our use of cookies. Alternatively, You can manage them in Your browser settings.
