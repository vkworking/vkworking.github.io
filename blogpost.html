UNIQUE HANDMADE BAGS VS MASS MARKET BY LOUIS VUITTON
21.08.2014
NASTYA MELNIK

Remember how the story of the great brand begins?! Everything started with an attempt to make life of travelers easier! Far back in the IXX century carpenter's son Louis invented the first light horizontal suitcase and from that the story for which people around the world pay starts! Yes, they are paying for the brand history, and only a small part from that amount - for the bag itself! In length of time, the practicality and convenience of bags were pushed aside and the exclusivity and quality were started to sell! Everything was easy, at those times these two concepts did not exclude but rather imply each other. It has been many many years, the history is still selling, the quality is also selling, the problem is only with exclusivity.

Leather bag with long handle

$129.00

Have you ever wondered why there is no discounts on bags in Louis Vuitton stores?! And well-known brand does not care is a collection a new or obsolete one. The reason is if someone else is able to afford this bag during a discount period, then that small privileged group, which is the "honorary frequenters" will doubt in its uniqueness! The problem is that this group is anyway becoming more and more wider due to thousands of fakes with different quality and price variations. And then it turns out that "honorary frequenters" do not pay for the uniqueness, quality or convenience, but for the fact that they know for sure, and sometimes only they know, that their bag is genuine! But all things change, and now really "unique" things become very popular! Hand-made bags, but for lower price! With increasingly frequency you can see girls with knit bags, embroidered handbags, bags with unusual decorative trim or even with bags, that look like antique, like only yesterday shepherds or residents of cold Finland wore them. These bags are gaining more and more popularity, because, firstly, they are of a good quality, and secondly, they are very comfortable, and of course they reflect the owner individuality.

Leather bag with stamping

$65.00

To be honest, I had always preferred the factorial bags of a good quality, but this current wave swept over me and bestowed several stunning instances that I use with great pleasure! I can be sure that I do not meet a girl with the same bag that often happened to me before! We are moving to a new level, we do not want to be like everyone else anymore! We just want to be ourself, to be unique and inimitable.
